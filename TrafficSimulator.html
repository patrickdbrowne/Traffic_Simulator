How do Traffic Simulations Work?






Traffic simulations involve the mathematical modeling of transportation systems through the application of computer software to better help plan, design, and operate transportation systems. This program visually demonstrates the interactions between individual elements of transportation systems, which are affected by:












  • Speed limits, which indicates the maximum speed vehicles can travel at;
  • Car density, which is the number of cars per km squared affecting traffic flow;
  • Critical gap, which is the distance between the vehicles; and
  • Aggression, which determines the probability of angry drivers beeping their horns....
